My Favorite Study Materials

My website will feature a curated collection of my favorite links to study materials. I plan to organize them by subject area—such as math, programming, and language learning— and provide a brief explanation for each link, highlighting what it offers and why it’s helpful. This setup will allow visitors to quickly locate and evaluate the resources they need, making it easier to study more effectively.

Math

  • Khan Academy – A wide range of free math tutorials and exercises.
  • Desmos – A powerful online graphing calculator for exploring functions and equations.

Programming

  • freeCodeCamp – Interactive lessons and projects for learning web development.
  • W3Schools – Tutorials and references for HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more.

Language Learning

  • Duolingo – A fun, gamified platform for learning new languages.
  • Memrise – Helps you expand your vocabulary through spaced repetition.
